Open GET:http://localhost:8081/actuator to see all actuator mappings

e.g.

http://localhost:8081/beans to show all your beans in the app

and the very nice
GET to see the entire documentation regarding the actuator
http://localhost:8081/docs

GET to see the configured loggers in the application
http://localhost:8081/loggers

or
GET to see the configured logger for a part of the application
http://localhost:8081/loggers/com.capgemini
